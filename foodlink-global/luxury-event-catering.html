A Symphony of Flavour and Hospitality

Foodlink Luxury Event Catering, the flagship division of the Foodlink Group, is known for crafting personalized and memorable experiences. We recognize that each client has unique needs, which allows us to tailor our approach for every event. From intimate gatherings to grand weddings, corporate functions to luxury weddings and sporting events, our dedicated team works closely with you to ensure that every detail aligns with your style, personality and brand. 

With over two decades of experience, we have redefined global catering standards by introducing acclaimed chefs, innovative service methods, and ISO-certified quality. Our dedication to excellence has earned us numerous accolades, establishing us as a leader in luxury event catering. Foodlink operates in four distinct segments, each offering specialized catering experiences.

Foodlink Weddings specializes in luxury wedding experiences with world-class culinary craftsmanship and meticulous attention to detail, blending innovation with tradition. Foodlink Corporate focuses on delivering efficient and solution-driven catering for MICE events and corporate functions, offering delicious, timely, and cost-effective meals that elevate the overall event experience. Foodlink Sports offers bespoke catering for sports events, focusing on the diverse nutritional needs of athletes and attendees to ensure a memorable dining experience. Lastly, Foodlink Boutique offers bespoke, intimate dining experiences, crafting personalized, exquisite gastronomical journeys for small gatherings with curated menus and refined presentations. Each segment excels in its respective niche, bringing unparalleled quality and expertise to its clients.
