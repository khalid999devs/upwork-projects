Diversity, Equity & Inclusion at Foodlink

We believe that a thriving workplace is one where diversity is celebrated, equity is prioritized, and inclusion is deeply embedded in everything we do. Our commitment is to create an environment where every individual feels seen, heard, and empowered to reach their fullest potential.

We are proud to be an equal opportunity employer, welcoming talented individuals from all walks of life. We do not discriminate on the basis of race, color, religion, gender, sexual orientation, nationality, genetics, disability, age, veteran status, or any other protected characteristic. Throughout the hiring journey and beyond, we uphold the highest standards in data privacy and respect for personal information.

At Foodlink, diversity isn’t just a value—it’s a strength. We embrace the unique backgrounds, perspectives, and identities of our people, and we ensure that their voices are not only heard but valued across all levels of the organization.

Our Culture

Foodlink's culture is the cornerstone of our success, a dynamic blend of creativity, collaboration, and purpose that fuels everything we do. It binds us together as one team, working with passion and precision towards a shared mission.

With a growing family of over 1,500 employees and associates, we’ve cultivated a workplace that’s inclusive, inspiring, and growth oriented. We intentionally hire individuals who resonate with our vision and values—people who are not just skilled, but also curious, driven, and open to new ideas.

We’re committed to nurturing talent and unlocking potential. From learning and development programs to career advancement opportunities, we empower our people to thrive, celebrate their milestones, and champion their aspirations.

At Foodlink, every voice matters. Every idea counts. And every person plays a role in shaping a brighter, bolder future. Together, we are more creative, more resilient, and more powerful in achieving lasting success.

Beware of Fraudulent Recruiting!

We have been made aware that some individuals have recently received fraudulent emails, phone calls, or other communications claiming to offer job opportunities at Foodlink Group. These scams typically ask candidates to deposit money into a bank account or send payments before moving forward with the recruitment process.

Please Note:

  • Foodlink Group never charges any fees at any stage of the recruitment or selection process.
  • Any request for money transfers, deposits, or financial details in relation to employment is fraudulent and not from us.
  • We urge you not to respond to such communications or disclose any personal or financial information.

If you have received a suspicious message or want to verify the legitimacy of a job offer, please reach out to us directly through our official communication channels.

Foodlink Group will not be held liable for any loss or damage arising from these fraudulent recruitment activities.

Stay alert. Stay protected.
