The Ultimate Pan-Asian Dining Experience

China Bistro offers an exceptional dining experience, bringing together the finest Asian flavours in a warm and inviting setting, perfect for gatherings with family and friends.

It takes you on a culinary journey across Asia, from the bustling streets of China, Korea, and Hong Kong to the vibrant marketplaces of Thailand, Indonesia, and Japan. With a modern twist on traditional recipes, our chefs craft dishes that are as imaginative as they are delicious, allowing the finest ingredients to shine in every bite.

Since opening our first location in Mumbai in 2012, China Bistro has quickly become a beloved destination for food lovers seeking a flavourful adventure through Asian cuisine. We take pride in breathing new life into age-old family recipes, creating dishes that reflect the diverse cultures and rich history of the region. Whether coastal, forested, tropical, or mountainous, each culture in Asia contributes its unique tastes and aromas to our menu, ensuring every meal is a sensory celebration.
