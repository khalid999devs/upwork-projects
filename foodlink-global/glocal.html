Where Global Flavours Meet Local Charm in a Stylish and Contemporary Setting

Glocal is a vibrant all-day dining and bar concept that seamlessly blends the global and local influences. Throughout the day, the restaurant offers a serene and elegant atmosphere, ideal for a leisurely meal, before transforming into a vibrant and high-energy lounge as the evening sets in. Designed to cater to the modern "global-local" generation— those who embody a global outlook while staying connected to their local roots.

At Glocal, our menu is a dynamic fusion of global inspiration and local soul. We don’t just blend cuisines—we reimagine iconic dishes through a uniquely Glocal perspective. Every plate is a thoughtful creation, marrying international techniques with regional ingredients to deliver flavours that feel both refreshingly new and deeply familiar. The result is an innovative dining experience that celebrates the best of both worlds.

Our bar menu is equally thoughtful, reflecting the sophisticated palates of today’s well-travelled guests. While it showcases global product knowledge and execution, the bar also embraces the playful and familiar elements of local culture, offering drinks that bring a sense of nostalgia alongside new and exciting concoctions. This perfect balance makes our bar a fun yet refined space where patrons can unwind and indulge.

The interior of Glocal is designed to reflect a blend of global techniques and local materials, capturing the beauty of simplicity, highlighting humble materials transformed into elegant, timeless design elements. This thoughtfully curated environment creates an inviting, and warm ambience. Glocal is a celebration of cultural convergence, where every visit promises a unique and memorable experience.
