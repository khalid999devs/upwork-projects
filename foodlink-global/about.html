Conception

Founded in 2003 by Sanjay Vazirani, Foodlink has evolved from a small event catering service in Mumbai to a global leader in luxury Food & Beverage solutions, offering a wide range of services across four key verticals Luxury Event Catering, Luxury Banquets & Conventions, Restaurants & Cloud Kitchens and Integrated F&B Services.

Driven by a passion for culinary excellence and a relentless pursuit of innovation, Foodlink has established itself as a pioneer known for creating unforgettable, bespoke culinary experiences for elite business houses, celebrities, and discerning clients worldwide. Our team adheres to international standards and best practices, skilfully blending global flavours with local tastes to create unique and memorable culinary experiences.

At Foodlink, we are committed to delivering exceptional quality and prioritizing customer satisfaction on every occasion. Headquartered in Dubai, UAE, Foodlink proudly serves both domestic and global markets, overseeing our operations worldwide. With a rich global footprint across over 25 countries, our extensive services cater to diverse needs. Our locations include Dubai (UAE), Antalya (Turkey), and Milan (Italy).

In India, our regional offices and state-of-the-art kitchens are strategically located in key cities, such as Mumbai (our India headquarter), Delhi, Ahmedabad, Hyderabad, Bangalore, and Chandigarh.
Our focus on innovation ensures that we stay at the forefront of industry trends, setting new benchmarks in the F&B service domain. We are also deeply committed to sustainability and community support, with efforts to minimize our environmental footprint while supporting local communities through various initiatives.

Foodlink remains dedicated to delivering unparalleled culinary artistry, professionalism, and service across diverse formats.
