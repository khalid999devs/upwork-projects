Where Tradition Meets Innovation in Every Bite

At India Bistro, every dish is a labour of love, created using the finest ingredients and innovative techniques to deliver both the purity of traditional flavours and the creativity of modern presentation. With a deep commitment to showcasing the essence of India’s 5000-year-old diverse cultural and food history, we offer a dining experience that is as much about heritage as it is about innovation.

India Bistro brings the rich, vibrant flavours of Indian cuisine to life with a contemporary twist. Our carefully curated menu offers a journey through the diverse culinary landscapes of India, from the heart of Amritsar to the edges of Waga and Lucknow, all while honouring the authenticity and depth of each region’s traditions.

In addition to our thoughtfully crafted dishes, our talented bartenders delight in creating exotic cocktails and mocktails that perfectly complement your meal. From the Kala Khatta Mojito to the Vodka Guppa, each drink is an experience by itself, designed to enhance the vibrant flavours of your meal.

Our vision is to offer an exceptional dining experience in a contemporary and elegant setting. Whatever the occasion, India Bistro is the perfect place for creating lasting memories.
