Where Good Food Sparks Meaningful Connections

We began our journey in restaurant business in 2012, and within a short time created four vibrant brands in the casual dining sector. With over 33 outlets across India and the UAE— India Bistro, China Bistro, Glocal, and Art of Dum.—we've earned a reputation for excellence.

Each of our brands has carved out a unique identity, offering everything from traditional North Indian and Chinese dishes to innovative World Fusion and Dum Pukht specialties. Our commitment to authenticity, creativity, and exceptional flavours has made our brands synonymous with culinary excellence.

At the heart of Foodlink is a mission to bring people together through unforgettable gourmet experiences. We use only the finest ingredients to craft every dish, ensuring that each visit to our restaurants is not just a meal, but a memorable occasion. Our team values you, which is why we go above and beyond to ensure that every aspect of your visit exceeds expectations, leaving you with an experience worth cherishing.

As we continue to expand, we remain deeply passionate about delivering exceptional dining environments and fostering lasting connections with every guest.
