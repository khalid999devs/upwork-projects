Seamless F&B solutions tailored for large scale Convention and Exhibition Centres

With a deep understanding of experiential spaces, and the ever-evolving landscape of the F&B sector, we offer end-to-end solutions for managing food and beverage services at large convention and exhibition centres.

We offer comprehensive banqueting and catering services that enhance the experience of attendees, exhibitors, and organisers. In addition to large-scale catering, we manage dynamic food court setups during exhibitions and events, featuring a mix of kiosks, vending machines and pop-up outlets from both our owned brands and renowned international names. Additionally, we also offer F&B retail spaces like Restaurants, Cafes and ‘Grab & Go format’ at strategic locations.

Our partnership with prestigious venues such as IICC, Yashbhoomi, Delhi; Nehru Centre, Mumbai, and World Trade Centre; Dubai stands as a testament to our ability to deliver excellence across diverse settings, providing seamless, exceptional F&B services to a global audience. At IICC, we have had the honour of hosting distinguished dignitaries, including Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi, and other prominent political figures and business leaders, furthering India’s ambition of becoming a global hub for MICE (Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, and Exhibitions). Partner with us for an unparalleled F&B experience that leaves a lasting impression.
